📘 TIMWOOD Definition

  • T - Transportation
    Unnecessary movement of information or documents
  • I - Inventory
    Work waiting to be processed, work in queue
  • M - Motion
    Unnecessary movement by people
  • W - Waiting
    Delays between steps, idle time
  • O - Overproduction
    Producing more or earlier than needed
  • O - Overprocessing
    More effort than required
  • D - Defect
    Errors causing rework

📋 Scenario Cards

How to Play

  1. Match all 24 scenario cards correctly before time runs out.
  2. Game time limit: 5 minutes (300 seconds).
  3. Review the Scenario Cards at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Drag a scenario card and drop it onto the matching waste category on the BINGO board.
  5. The center STEA square is a FREE space.
  6. Complete rows, columns, or diagonals to earn 🎯 BINGO lines.
  7. Wrong placements increase your ❌ Wrong Attempts count.
  8. Your final result is based on:
    • Correct Matches
    • BINGO Lines Achieved
    • Completion Time
  9. 🔄 Reset: Restart current game (cards reshuffled). 🚪 Quit: Return to login screen.
💡 Tips: Use the TIMWOOD definitions and waste-identification tips shown beside the BINGO board.
